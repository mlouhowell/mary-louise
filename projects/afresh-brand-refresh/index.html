Intro

In 2022, I worked on a brand refresh for Afresh, a fresh technology company serving grocery retailers. The objective was to position Afresh as a premium alternative within its competitive landscape.

I collaborated with design studio Upper Quad, offering directional feedback throughout development. Once the creative direction solidified, the work expanded across multiple mediums and departments.

Scope

Print advertising, a 20' × 20' exhibition booth, brand imagery, whitepapers, patterns and promotional items, microsites, corporate events, greeting cards, video graphics, and comprehensive brand guidelines.

Collaboration

Marketing, engineering, and product teams worked together to systematically implement the refreshed brand identity across all customer touchpoints.
