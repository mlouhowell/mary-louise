Intro

Postly lets you add photos and text to a canvas and share them with friends. When shared, the post gets saved to your own personal feed as a log of your digital postcards.

Postly Desktop App

I'm currently exploring Postly as a desktop app for more flexibility in editing a canvas. I've been experimenting with Pretext and how text can be designed on the page alongside images.

Process

I built this app in one weekend using sketches and designs in Paper. A custom handwritten font was developed with a few screenshots and some manual vector editing in Figma.

Upcoming features

AI image generation, AI layout suggestions, and the ability to create your own handwritten font.
